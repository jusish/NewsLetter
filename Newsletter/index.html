Nigeria

Nigeria, an African country on the Gulf of Guinea, has many natural landmarks and wildlife reserves. Protected areas such as Cross River National Park and Yankari National Park have waterfalls, dense rainforest, savanna and rare primate habitats. One of the most recognizable sites is Zuma Rock, a 725m-tall monolith outside the capital of Abuja that is pictured on the national currency.

Airports

Kanombe International Airport is the main international airport serving Kigali, the capital city of Rwanda. It is also known as Kigali International Airport. The airport is located in the suburb of Kanombe, approximately 5 kilometers east of the city center. Kanombe Airport is the largest and busiest airport in Rwanda, serving as a hub for both domestic and international flights.

Radison Blu Hotel

Radisson Blu is an international chain of hotels operated by Radisson Hotels. With roots dating back to the 1960s, the Radisson Blu brand name came into existence in 2009 with a rebranding from Radisson SAS. Its hotels are found in major cities, key airport gateways and leisure destinations.
